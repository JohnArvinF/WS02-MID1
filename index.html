About Hanako or Yugi Amane

Hanako (花はな子こ Hanako?), or as a human Yugi Amane (柚ゆ木ぎ普あまね Yugi Amane?) ,is a ghost in the old building of Kamome Academy who grants wishes and governs over the Seven Mysteries as School Mystery Number 7.

Amane characteristics

  1. He's is short boy
  2. He's resemble crescent moons.
  3. Hanako is cheerful, outgoing, and likes having fun.
